How to read this

  • The dashed divider splits the sorted left side from the unsorted right side.
  • The orange key is the value currently being inserted into the sorted side.
  • Red bars shift right to make space for the key.
  • When little shifting is needed, Insertion Sort behaves close to O(n); heavy shifting leads to O(n^2).
